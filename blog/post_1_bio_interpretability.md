## The Question

I've spent my career finding the "unit of representation" in biological systems — first in neural circuits at UCLA's Mehta Lab, where I recorded place cells to understand how the brain encodes space, then in cancer biology, where I mapped nucleotide metabolism to understand how tumors survive replication stress. Now at Kite Pharma, I identify biomarkers that predict whether a patient's CAR T-cells will fight their lymphoma or fail.

In every case, the core challenge is the same: you have a complex system producing observable outputs, and you need to understand the internal computation that generates them.

Transformer language models are the next system I want to decode.
